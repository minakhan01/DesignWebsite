Design Highlights

Mind Time

Our perception of time is complex and is computed in different parts of our brain.

Emotions & time

Our emotions are connected to our time perception. Positive emotions speed up time, and negative emotions slow down time.

Cognitive state & time

When we're focused/engaged, time goes faster and when we're bored/relaxed, time goes slowly.

CAD model

The goal was that EMIT would look like a normal clock. This is the design I made to house the strobe LEDs, motor, Neopixels, and the real clock.

Initial design

The side view shows the extra strobe hand behind the clock hands. The LED lights go around the inner side of the rim.

LED in the case

Strobing is done using a LED strip which goes around the sides of the clock.

Modified design

I made the sides slanted so that the light is more directed on the clock hand.

Woodwork

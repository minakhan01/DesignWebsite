Time Perception

Overview

This week I spent a lot of time storyboarding and trying to craft a story for my video. I wrote a script for my video and then storyboarded the shots I would take during my video. I composed the motivation video below according to points 1 to 4 of my script. The rest of the points need footage of the real clock, which I storyboarded. In short, I have five major components this week:

  1. Possible names: Brainstorm names and logos
  2. Script: describes plan for entire video (10 points)
  3. Motivation video: The video according to points 1 to 4 of the script. This doesn't need footage from the clock, so I could make the video beforehand
  4. Storyboard: I need to shoot the shots for points 5 to 10 in the script, so I story boarded the shots.
  5. Research: I read the book 'Time Wraped' to get better perspective on time perception. I attached some screeenshots of my notes.
